## What We Make

Single-serve sachets of functional matcha blended with organic mushroom extracts and grass-fed collagen peptides. Two flavors: Vanilla and Strawberry.

**One sentence:** Cafe-quality functional matcha you make at home in 30 seconds.

---

## Brand Positioning

> "Cafe energy. Home address. No crash."

shroome sits at the intersection of three trends:
1. **Functional beverages** — consumers want their drinks to do something
2. **At-home convenience** — the cafe habit is expensive and time-consuming
3. **Ingredient transparency** — people are reading labels and rejecting proprietary blends

We are not a supplement company that happens to taste okay. We are a drink brand that happens to be functional.

---

## Key Differentiator

**70%+ beta-glucan content in our mushroom extracts.**

Most mushroom supplements on the market use mycelium-on-grain, which contains 15-30% beta-glucans diluted with starch. Our extracts are fruiting-body-derived, hot-water-extracted, and third-party verified at 70%+ beta-glucan concentration.

This is not a marginal difference — it is a fundamentally different product category.

## Target Customer

**Primary:** Health-conscious young adults (22-35) who currently spend $5-7/day on cafe drinks and are interested in functional ingredients but overwhelmed by the supplement aisle.

**Secondary:** Fitness and wellness enthusiasts who want clean-label products with real doses, not marketing fluff.

**Psychographic:** Values transparency, reads labels, skeptical of wellness hype, wants to feel good without a complicated routine.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Waitlist & Community (Current)
- Build waitlist via drinkshroome.com
- Email nurture flows educating subscribers on ingredients and formulation
- Organic social content across four pillars (education, lifestyle, transparency, community)
- 20% off + free shipping launch discount locked in for all waitlist subscribers (extra 10% for phone number, stackable)

### Phase 2: DTC Launch
- Launch on drinkshroome.com (Shopify or headless commerce)
- Waitlist-first access window (48-72 hours exclusive)
- Subscription model: monthly delivery with discount incentive
- Single-purchase option for trial

### Phase 3: Expansion
- Cafe and coffee shop partnerships (B2B sachets for menu integration)
- Retail exploration (specialty grocery, wellness retailers)
- Flavor line extensions based on customer feedback
- Potential ready-to-drink (RTD) format for retail

## Brand Values

1. **Transparency** — Every ingredient, every dose, on the label. No proprietary blends.
2. **Quality over quantity** — Fewer SKUs, better formulation. We would rather do two flavors exceptionally than ten flavors adequately.
3. **Honesty** — We make structure/function claims we can back up. We do not promise miracles.
4. **Accessibility** — Functional wellness should not require a $200/month supplement stack.
5. **Energy** — We are a young brand. Our aesthetic, voice, and community reflect that.
