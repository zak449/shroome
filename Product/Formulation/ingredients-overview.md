## Formulation Philosophy

Every ingredient in shroome is there for a reason, at a dose that matters. No proprietary blends. No fairy-dusting. No filler. The full ingredient list and doses are printed on every sachet because transparency is not a marketing angle — it is the standard.

## Core Ingredients

### 1. Ceremonial Matcha — 2g per sachet

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Grade | Ceremonial |
| Harvest | First harvest (ichiban-cha) |
| Cultivation | Shade-grown (minimum 21 days under cover) |
| Caffeine | ~50mg per serving |
| L-Theanine | Naturally occurring (~30-40mg) |

**Why it matters:**
- First-harvest, shade-grown matcha has higher L-theanine content than culinary grades, which promotes calm focus rather than jittery energy
- 2g is a meaningful ceremonial serving — many competitors use 1-2g of culinary-grade matcha and call it "matcha-powered"
- The L-theanine + caffeine combination produces sustained alertness without the crash associated with coffee
- ~50mg caffeine is roughly half a shot of espresso, delivered with a smoother absorption curve

**Taste contribution:** Vegetal sweetness, umami depth, the "green" backbone of the drink

---

### 2. Organic Mushroom Extracts — 200mg per sachet

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Source | Fruiting body extract (not mycelium-on-grain) |
| Beta-glucan content | 70%+ (verified via third-party testing) |
| Form | Hot water extracted, spray-dried powder |

**Why it matters:**
- **70%+ beta-glucan content is the key differentiator.** Most mushroom supplements on the market contain 15-30% beta-glucans. Many use mycelium-on-grain, which dilutes active compounds with starch filler
- Beta-glucans are the primary bioactive compound in functional mushrooms — they are what drive immune-modulating and adaptogenic benefits
- Fruiting body extracts contain significantly higher concentrations of beta-glucans than mycelium products
- Hot water extraction is the traditional and most effective method for liberating beta-glucans from mushroom cell walls (chitin)

**Functional role:** Immune support, sustained focus, adaptogenic stress response

---

### 3. Grass-Fed Collagen — 2g per sachet

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Source | Grass-fed, pasture-raised bovine |
| Form | Hydrolyzed collagen peptides |
| Molecular weight | Low molecular weight for bioavailability |
| Type | Primarily Type I and Type III |

**Why it matters:**
- Hydrolyzed collagen peptides are broken down into smaller molecules that the body can absorb more efficiently than whole collagen
- 2g is a meaningful daily maintenance dose — while clinical studies often use 5-10g, daily consistency at 2g contributes to cumulative benefits
- Type I collagen supports skin elasticity and hydration; Type III supports gut lining and skin structure
- Grass-fed sourcing ensures no added hormones or antibiotics in the supply chain

**Functional role:** Skin health support, gut lining support, daily collagen maintenance

## Flavor Profiles

### Vanilla
- **Character:** Warm, floral, grounding
- **Experience:** Feels like a latte — smooth, comforting, slightly sweet
- **Best for:** People who love oat milk lattes, chai, warm beverages with depth
- **Flavor notes:** Madagascar vanilla, subtle caramel warmth, creamy finish

### Strawberry
- **Character:** Bright, fruity, fresh
- **Experience:** Feels like a refreshing morning smoothie — light and energizing
- **Best for:** People who want something less traditional, fruit-forward, summery
- **Flavor notes:** Real strawberry, light tartness, clean finish without artificial sweetness

## What's NOT in shroome

| Excluded | Reason |
|---|---|
| Added sugar | Unnecessary calories; natural sweetness from matcha and flavoring |
| Artificial sweeteners | Conflicts with clean-label positioning |
| Proprietary blends | Every ingredient and dose is disclosed |
| Mycelium-on-grain | Low beta-glucan content, starch filler |
| Artificial colors | No need — matcha provides natural color |
| Preservatives | Single-serve sachets eliminate the need for preservatives |
| Soy or dairy | Allergen-friendly; collagen is the only animal-derived ingredient |

## Preparation

1. Open sachet and pour contents into a mug or shaker bottle
2. Add 6-8oz of hot water (not boiling — 170-180F is ideal for matcha)
3. Stir or shake until fully dissolved
4. Optional: add milk of choice for a latte-style drink
5. Drink within 30 minutes for best flavor and texture

---

## Storage & Shelf Life

- Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
- Shelf life: 18 months from production date (sealed)
- Once opened, consume immediately (single-serve format)

## Certifications & Testing

- Third-party tested for beta-glucan content (70%+ verified)
- Heavy metals testing (arsenic, lead, cadmium, mercury)
- Microbial testing (total plate count, yeast, mold, coliforms)
- Manufactured in a GMP-certified facility
