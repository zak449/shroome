## Script 1: "The 15-Second Matcha Latte" (ASMR)

**Pillar:** Lifestyle / Energy
**Duration:** 15 seconds
**Style:** No voiceover. Pure ASMR. All sound design.

### Hook (0-2s)
Close-up macro shot of fingers gripping the sachet notch. The tear sound is crisp and loud.

### Shot List

| Time | Shot | Audio |
|------|------|-------|
| 0-2s | Extreme close-up: fingers tear sachet along the perforation. Powder visible inside. | Amplified tear sound. No music yet. |
| 2-5s | Overhead: powder streams out of sachet into a clear glass filled with oat milk. Slow, controlled pour. | Soft powder-hitting-liquid sound. Gentle ambient tone fades in. |
| 5-9s | Side angle through glass: matcha plume blooms and swirls through the milk. Green ribbons curling downward. No stirring yet. | Lo-fi ambient pad builds slightly. |
| 9-12s | Close-up: a single slow stir with a spoon. The drink turns from streaked to uniform sage green. | Spoon-against-glass clink. |
| 12-15s | Beauty shot: glass on a sunlit surface, condensation on the outside, hand reaches in and lifts it. Hold for 2 beats. | Music resolves. Silence on the sip. |

### Text Overlays
- (0s) — *nothing. let the tear speak.*
- (5s) — "15 seconds."
- (7s) — "No whisk. No blender."
- (12s) — "Cafe-grade matcha."
- (14s) — "shroome" + logo

### Music
Lo-fi ambient / soft piano. Think "study beats but make it elegant." No vocals. Keep it quiet enough that ASMR sounds dominate.

### Hashtags
`#matcha #matchalatte #asmr #matchaasmr #morningroutine #matchatok #oatmilk #shroome #functionalfood #nowhiskneeded`

## Production Notes (All Scripts)

**Filming specs:**
- Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920 minimum, 4K preferred)
- Natural light preferred. No harsh overhead fluorescents.
- Product should always be in warm, real-life settings (kitchen counter, desk, windowsill). No studio white backgrounds.

**Brand consistency:**
- The tear-pour-done sequence appears in every script. This is the signature visual. Film it the same way each time so it becomes recognizable.
- Use the actual shroome sachet. No mockups.
- Oat milk is the default pairing in all videos. Almond or regular milk are acceptable alternates.

**Posting cadence:**
- One script per week. Stagger across TikTok (post first) and Instagram Reels (post 24-48 hours later).
- Post TikTok between 7-9am or 6-8pm local time for highest engagement.

**Caption formula:**
Hook line from script + one sentence of value + CTA ("Link in bio" or "Save this for later") + hashtags.

**Compliance:**
- No disease claims. Use structure/function language only.
- Personal experience content (Script 4) must include "individual results may vary" disclaimer.
- See `Product/Compliance & Claims/claims-guidelines.md` for full guidelines.
