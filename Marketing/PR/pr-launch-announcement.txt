SHROOMÉ INTRODUCES WORLD'S FIRST READY-TO-POUR CEREMONIAL MATCHA LATTE

New liquid sachet format combines ceremonial grade matcha, functional mushroom extracts and collagen peptides — prepared in 15 seconds flat

UNITED STATES — Shroomé today announced the launch of the world's first ready-to-pour ceremonial matcha latte, a liquid sachet format that delivers café-quality matcha with functional benefits in 15 seconds. The product enters a functional beverage market projected to exceed $4.5 billion globally, targeting health-conscious consumers who want premium ingredients without the preparation complexity.

Each Shroomé sachet contains 2 grams of ceremonial grade matcha that is shade-grown for a minimum of 21 days and stone-ground, 200 milligrams of functional mushroom extracts from Lion's Mane and Reishi fruiting bodies, and 2 grams of grass-fed bovine collagen peptides featuring Types I and III. The pre-dissolved liquid format eliminates the need for whisking, sifting or specialized equipment. Users simply tear, pour into milk and drink.

The product launches in two flavors: Vanilla Oat and Strawberry Rose. Shroomé is currently in pre-launch phase, collecting waitlist signups at drinkshroome.com ahead of its initial production run.

"The functional beverage space is full of powders that clump, require blenders or taste like supplements," said Zachary Kaufman, founder of Shroomé. "We built a format that solves the preparation problem without compromising on ingredient quality. Every sachet uses the same ceremonial grade matcha you'd find in a high-end café, paired with clinically relevant doses of mushroom extracts and collagen."

Kaufman pointed to a gap between consumer demand for functional ingredients and the inconvenience of existing delivery formats. According to industry data, matcha sales in the United States have grown at a compound annual growth rate of 9.5 percent, driven largely by younger consumers seeking sustained energy without the crash associated with coffee. Yet traditional matcha preparation requires a bamboo whisk, proper water temperature and technique that most consumers lack.

"We are not asking people to change their morning routine," Kaufman said. "We are replacing it with something faster that delivers more. Two grams of ceremonial matcha, mushroom extracts standardized to over 70 percent beta-glucans, and collagen — all in one pour."

The company plans to announce retail pricing and shipping timelines in the coming weeks. Early access will be offered to waitlist subscribers.
