Hey Product Hunt! I'm Zach, founder of ZSQUARED INC and creator of shroomé.

I spent the last year trying to solve a simple problem: I wanted a daily matcha latte with functional ingredients, but the process was annoying. Powder clumps, you need a whisk or frother, cleanup takes longer than drinking it, and if you want to add mushroom extracts and collagen, that's three separate products and even more prep time.

shroomé is a liquid concentrate in a single-serve sachet. You tear it open, pour it over ice and milk, and you have a full ceremonial matcha latte with mushroom extracts and collagen in under 15 seconds. No tools, no mess, no compromise on ingredient quality.

A few things we're proud of:

We're currently in pre-launch and offering 20% off + free shipping for early supporters. Would love to hear your thoughts, questions, and honest feedback.

## 3 Key Features

### 1. Ready-to-Pour Format
No powder, no frother, no whisk, no hot water step. Tear open the sachet and pour directly over ice and milk. A full matcha latte in 15 seconds. This is the first liquid-concentrate format for ceremonial matcha.

### 2. Three-in-One Functional Stack
Each sachet combines 2.5g ceremonial matcha (60mg caffeine + natural L-theanine), organic mushroom extracts standardized to 70%+ beta-glucans, and 2g grass-fed collagen peptides. Three products in one pour.

### 3. Ingredient Transparency
No proprietary blends. Every ingredient and dose is printed on the sachet. Mushroom extracts are fruiting body only (not mycelium-on-grain). Beta-glucan content is third-party verified. Matcha is first-harvest ceremonial grade.

## Launch Day Strategy

### Pre-Launch (1 week before)

- [ ] Schedule launch date on Product Hunt (aim for Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday — highest engagement days)
- [ ] Prepare all assets: logo, gallery images (sachet product shots, pour sequence, latte result), maker profile photo
- [ ] Write and finalize the first comment (above)
- [ ] Brief 10-15 supporters to be ready to upvote and leave genuine comments in the first 2 hours (the early engagement window is critical for PH algorithm)
- [ ] Prepare 3-4 discussion prompts/questions to post in comments if engagement slows
- [ ] Create a Product Hunt-exclusive offer (e.g., "PH community gets early access + 25% off first box")

### Launch Day

- [ ] Launch at 12:01 AM PT (Product Hunt resets daily at midnight Pacific)
- [ ] Post the first comment immediately after launch goes live
- [ ] Share launch link on personal social media, email list, and Discord/Slack communities within the first hour
- [ ] Send a dedicated email to the pre-launch waitlist: "We just launched on Product Hunt — support us!"
- [ ] Monitor comments and respond to every single one within 30 minutes (engagement rate matters for ranking)
- [ ] Post 2-3 updates throughout the day as comment threads develop (behind-the-scenes, formulation details, founder story)
- [ ] Cross-post to Twitter/X with the PH launch link and tag @ProductHunt

### Post-Launch (48 hours after)

- [ ] Thank everyone who supported on PH with a follow-up comment
- [ ] Send a follow-up email to the waitlist with launch results and social proof ("We hit #X on Product Hunt!")
- [ ] Compile all feedback and questions from PH comments into a document for product/marketing review
- [ ] Connect with anyone who left detailed feedback — potential early advocates or beta testers
- [ ] Write a short retrospective: what worked, what to improve for next launch

### Assets Needed

- [ ] Product Hunt logo (240x240, PNG, no rounded corners — PH will round them)

## Positioning Notes

Product Hunt audience skews technical, design-conscious, and ingredient-skeptical. Lead with the format innovation (liquid concentrate sachet) and ingredient transparency. Avoid wellness buzzwords. Lean into the functional/nootropic angle — this audience respects L-theanine + caffeine research. Be specific with numbers (70%+ beta-glucans, 60mg caffeine, 15 seconds). Back up claims with data, not vibes.
