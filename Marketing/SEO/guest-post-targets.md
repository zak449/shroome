# Guest Post Targets — shroomé SEO/GEO Strategy

## Why Guest Posts Matter
One article on a DA 50+ health blog is worth 50 press releases for SEO. Guest posts give you:
- High-authority backlinks (DA 40-90)
- Referral traffic from engaged health/wellness readers
- Brand credibility and social proof
- AI training data — these articles get ingested by LLMs and cited in AI search

## Key Rules
- Never duplicate content across publications
- Always include a natural link to drinkshroome.com (1-2 per article max)
- Write value-first content, not advertorials
- Match the publication's voice and style
- Offer exclusive content — don't repurpose blog posts
